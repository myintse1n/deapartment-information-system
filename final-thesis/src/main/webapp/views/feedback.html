A feedback system is a crucial component of an electronic department management system, particularly in educational institutions. It primarily aimed at improving the overall quality of education and ensuring that the teaching methods, skills, and knowledge imparted by teachers meet the standards and expectations of the institution and its students.

Feedback forms play a critical role in measuring teacher performance by allowing students to evaluate aspects such as subject knowledge, clarity in teaching, and overall methodology. This information helps management identify both strengths and areas needing improvement, ensuring high teaching standards. Constructive feedback also enables teachers to refine their methods, making their teaching more effective and better suited to students' needs.

Moreover, feedback helps assess teacher suitability for specific subjects, ensuring they are assigned roles where they can be most effective. It also promotes a culture of continuous improvement, guiding professional development and institutional enhancements. By valuing student input, the feedback system increases engagement and satisfaction, fostering a more responsive and inclusive educational environment. Therefore, it is crucial for students to regularly submit feedback forms each semester.
