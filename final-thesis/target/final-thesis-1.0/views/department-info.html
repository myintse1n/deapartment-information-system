Welcome to Electronic Engineering Department

Our department is a hub of innovation and excellence, committed to preparing future engineers and technologists to shape the evolving world of electronics and communication systems.

Our department offers comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to provide a solid foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Our curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including Digital and Analog Electronics, Microprocessor and Microcontroller Systems, Communication Systems, Signal Processing, Embedded Systems, VLSI Design, and Control Systems. Our aim is to equip you with the expertise needed to excel in diverse career paths within the electronics industry.

Laboratories and Facilities:

The department boasts state-of-the-art laboratories that are essential for hands-on learning and research. These include: Electronics Lab Microprocessor and Microcontroller Lab Communication Lab Digital Signal Processing Lab VLSI

Lab Research and Innovation:

Design The Electronics Department at MTU is actively involved in research activities. Faculty and students work on cutting-edge projects that contribute to advancements in electronics and communication technologies. Research areas include wireless communication, digital signal processing, and semiconductor technology.

Industry Collaboration:

The department maintains strong ties with the industry to ensure that the education provided is relevant to current technological trends. Collaboration with industry partners includes internships, joint research projects, and guest lectures from industry experts.

Student Activities:

Students in the Electronics Department are encouraged to participate in various extracurricular activities, including technical clubs, competitions, and seminars. These activities provide opportunities for practical learning, networking, and professional development.
